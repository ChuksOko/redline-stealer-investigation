08

Conclusion

Host 10.10.23.101 (machine: user1) was infected with RedLine Stealer after executing a malicious file disguised as a business quotation archive. The malware injected itself into the legitimate Windows process RegSvcs.exe to evade detection.

It established communication with C2 server 188.190.10.10:55123 using SOAP/XML over HTTP — a format normally associated with legitimate enterprise software — and successfully exfiltrated approximately 2.7 MB of sensitive user data including browser credentials, cryptocurrency wallets, and session tokens.

⚡ Historical Note: The RedLine Stealer operation was taken down by the FBI, Dutch National Police, and Eurojust on October 28, 2024 — just 5 days after this infection — in Operation Magnus.
